Space Paint

Welcome to the space laser control panel!

Here you can use our equatorial satellite constellation to control the worlds weather. Unfortunately, due to congressional budget cuts we were unable to get polar coverage.

Views

We have three view layers you can overlay. Cloud map, heat map, and wind map. Each view can be separately toggled, by default all three are enabled.

Controls

We have three weather elements you can control. Clouds, heat, and wind. Each control can be separately toggled between disabled, increase, and decrease. By default all three are disabled.
